Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1

Step 1: Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.

Step 2: Add milk and egg, then whisk until smooth.

Step 3: Pour batter onto a hot greased pan and cook until golden.

Creamy Alfredo Pasta

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 8 oz
Butter 1/4 cup
Parmesan 1 cup grated

Step 1: Cook pasta according to package instructions.

Step 2: Melt butter in a pan, then stir in Parmesan.

Step 3: Add cooked pasta and mix until creamy.
